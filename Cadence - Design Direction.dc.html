Cadence
Design direction · working concept v1
A language app built to make you speak

Duolingo builds a habit.
Cadence builds a speaker.

Duolingo wins the first 100 days — the streak, the dopamine, the league. Then learners hit a wall: endless translation drills, no real conversation, no path to fluency. Cadence starts where Duo stalls — free AI conversation from day one, real immersion, culture in every lesson, and motivation built on progress instead of guilt.
